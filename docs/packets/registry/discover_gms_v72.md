### Method

- **Clientbound universe** built by `packet-audit discover-ops --apply` switch-walking the
  curated v72 dispatcher set (into a throwaway registry to harvest the ground-truth
  `(opcode, FName, address)` triples), then joining each discovered opcode to the
  `gms_v79.yaml` FName→Op dictionary. The v72 clientbound opcode space is **non-uniformly**
  shifted from v79 — verified region deltas: CWvsContext/login **Δ0**, CField base **Δ−4**,
  mob **Δ−6→−8**, npc & lower map-pools **Δ−8**, high-region dialogs **Δ−12** — plus
  version-absent ops and a structurally-divergent `CCashShop` block. Opcodes were therefore
  **never copied from v79**; every one is read from the v72 switch.
- **Serverbound** derived from v72 `COutPacket(N)` send-sites. A global xref harvest of the
  `COutPacket::COutPacket(int)` constructor (`0x656fa1`) was walked to read the pushed opcode
  at **every** send-site in the binary; each of the 112 v79 serverbound ops was then matched
  to its v72 opcode and cross-checked against that harvest. **Verified region deltas:**
  login (v79 op ≤23) **Δ0**, mid/field/social (24–154) **Δ+1**, deep
  (pet/summon/mob/npc/reactor/minigame/cashshop, ≥163) **Δ−2**. There are no v79 serverbound
  ops in the 155–162 gap, so the +1↔−2 boundary is unambiguous. NPC_TALK (Stage-A UNVERIFIED)
  is resolved: `COutPacket(57)` in the harvest (v79 56, Δ+1).
