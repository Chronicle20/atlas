Abandoned Subway Party Quest
Requires at least 1 players to register. Participants must be between level 21 and 30.
Recruitment is started by talking to Mr. Lim (1052115) in Abandoned Subway Station (910320000).
Exit map - Abandoned Subway Station (910320000) exists.
Added in GMS Version 81

Stage 1 - Monster Killing 1
This stage takes place in Dusty Platform (910330100)
This stage ends in 3 minutes. When the timer reaches 0, the party will be warped to the next stage.
If the ACT bar reaches 0, the stage will be failed.
Party members may speak to Mr. Lim (1052115) to forfeit.

Stage 2 - Monster Killing 2
This stage takes place in Dusty Platform (910330200)
This stage ends in 3 minutes. When the timer reaches 0, the party will be warped to the next stage.
If the ACT bar reaches 0, the stage will be failed.
Party members may speak to Mr. Lim (1052115) to forfeit.

Stage 3 - Monster Killing 3
This stage takes place in Dusty Platform (910330300)
This stage ends in 3 minutes. When the timer reaches 0, the party will be warped to the next stage.
If the ACT bar reaches 0, the stage will be failed.
Party members may speak to Mr. Lim (1052115) to forfeit.

Stage 4 - Rewards
This stage takes place in Dusty Platform (910320001)
Party members will be graded on the number of kills completed during the party quest.
Party members may speak to Mr. Lim (1052115) to either enter the bonus stage, or exit. If the party member chooses to exit, they will receive a Train 999 Boarding Pass (4001321) which can be used at a later time to enter the bonus stage.

Stage 5 - Bonus
This stage takes place in Platform - Train 999 (910320010)
The party quest has cleared, party members have a limited time (1 minute) to collect Subway Lost and Found (2022615) from Metro Bubbling (9700020). When the time is up, the party is warped to the exit. Party members may exit via portal out00.
