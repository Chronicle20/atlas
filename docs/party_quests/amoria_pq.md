Amoria Party Quest
Requires 6 players, above level 40. All members of the party must be married (not necessarily to each other), and have an Entrance Ticket (4031592).
Recruitment is started by talking to Amos the Strong (9201048) in Entrance of Amorian Challenge (670010100).
You have 45 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Exit maps (670011000) exist.

Stage 1 - Cloud Piece Collection.
Takes place in Stage 1 - Magik Mirror (670010200)
Party speaks with Amos the Strong (9201044), who summons 3 portals. The correct portal is randomized. There are two portals for male and female characters, defeat the monsters. Have your party leader talk to The Glimmer Man (9201047). A magic fairy will be summoned, who drops the Wing Hammer (4031596). Use it (by dropping) to break the mirror (reactor 6702000) in the middle of the stage. Which drops the Magik Mirror Shard to complete the stage.
Party members are awarded 2000 experience points on stage completion.

Stage 2 - Heart Strings Combination Puzzle.
Takes place in one of 3 Stage 2 - Heart Strings (670010300, 670010301, or 670010302) maps depending on time of day.
Party members (5) must occupy three ropes to identify the correct combination. If you do not get it, in seven attempts, monsters are spawned, and the combination is changed. Combination guess is verified through Amos the Strong (9201044).
Party members are awarded 4000 experience points on stage completion.

Stage 3 - Twister Switcher Combination Puzzle.
Takes place in Stage 3 - Twisted Switcher (670010400)
Party members (5) must occupy five platforms. An incorrect guess will summon monsters (Slimes and Crystal Boars) with a hint towards how many correct platforms exist (# of Slimes). If you do not get it, in seven attempts, the combination is changed. Combination guess is verified through	Amos the Strong (9201044).
Party members are awarded 6000 experience points on stage completion.

Stage 4 - Last Man Standing
Takes place in Stage 4 - Last Man Standing (670010500)
Collect 50 Cupid Code Pieces (4031597) and exchanged with	Amos the Strong (9201045).
Party members are awarded 8000 experience points on stage completion.

Stage 5 - Fluttering Hearts
Takes place in Stage 5 - Fluttering Hearts (670010600)
Party members run to the right, breaking gates along the way.
Party members are awarded 9000 experience points on stage completion.

Stage 6 - Boss Stage
Takes place in Stage 6 - Love Hurts (670010700)
Kill all three phases of Geist Balrog to receive the Geist Fang (4031594).
Party members are awarded 11000 experience points on stage completion.

Stage 7 - Bonus Stage
Takes place in either Stage 7 - Amos' Vault (670010800) or Stage 7 - Amos' Vault for Couples (670010750) depending on if the party is filled with all three couples, or not.

For Amos' Vault:
The party quest has cleared, party members have a one minute to collect drops from boxes, and then they will be warped to the party quest exit.

For Amos' Vault for Couples:
The party is split into three teams (aligned by couple) to see who can collect 35 Cupid Code Pieces (4031597) first. The items drop from monsters who are summoned by breaking boxes (reactors) throughout the stage. One member, of the winning couple can exchange the Cupid Code Pieces with	Amos the Strong (9201045). For a random reward. One of (The Legendary Elias Cape 1	(1102101),The Legendary Elias Cape 2	(1102102), The Legendary Elias Cape 3	(1102103), Cecelia Cloak 1	(1102104), Cecelia Cloak 2	(1102105), or Cecelia Cloak 3	(1102106). After finishing this stage, the party members will be warped to the regular Amos' Vault, for the normal bonus stage.
