Crimsonwood Keep Party Quest
Requires at least 10 players, a maximum of 20 players, to register. Participants must be level 90 or higher.
The party must be composed of at least two of each job class, Bowman, Warrior, Magician, Thief, and Pirate.
Recruitment is started by talking to Jack (9201113) in Hall to Inner Sanctum (610030020).
Exit map - Hall of Mastery (610030010) exists.
This party quest was replaced in GMS v141.

Stage 1 - Inner Sanctum Hallway
This stage takes place in Inner Sanctum Hallway (610030100)
The party leader must talk to Jack (9201112) to trigger the reactor (6109010) allowing the party to proceed through portal next00 via portal script glpqPortal0
You have three minutes to complete this stage.
If the party lingers too long in this stage, Master Guardian (9400594) will be summoned.

Stage 2 - Forgotten Storage Chamber
This stage takes place in Forgotten Storage Chamber (610030200)
The party must trigger five reactors (6109000, 6109001, 6109002, 6109003, 6109004) using class specific skills. Once all five reactors are triggered, the party must proceed through portal next00 via portal script glpqPortal1
You have eight minutes to complete this stage.

Stage 3 - Test of Agility
This stage takes place in The Test of Agility (610030300)
The party must trigger five reactors (6109000, 6109001, 6109002, 6109003, 6109004) using class specific skills. Once all five reactors are triggered, five party members must trigger the portals at the top of the map to complete the stage. This will enable the portal next00 at the bottom of the map, and allow the party to proceed via portal script glpqPortal2
You have ten minutes to complete this stage.

Stage 4 - Test of Wit
This stage takes place in The Test of Wit (610030400)
The party must trigger five reactors (6109000, 6109001, 6109002, 6109003, 6109004) using class specific skills. There are fake reactors as well. This will enable the portal next00 at the bottom of the map, and allow the party to proceed via portal script glpqPortal4
You have ten minutes to complete this stage.

Stage 5 - Test of Unity
This stage takes place in The Test of Unity (610030500)
Each class has a portal they can enter. (glpqPortal00 - Warrior Mastery Room (610030510), glpqPortal01 - Mage Mastery Room (610030520), Mage Mastery Room (610030521), Mage Mastery Room (610030522), glpqPortal03 - Thief Mastery Room (610030530), glpqPortal04 - Bowman Mastery Room (610030540), or glpqPortal05 - Pirate Mastery Room (610030550)) to acquire a weapon once they complete the object. The weapons are dropped on reactors (6109005, 6109006, 6109007, 6109008, and 6109009) in the main room. Triggering all five reactors will enable the portal next00 at the bottom of the map, and allow the party to proceed via portal script glpqPortal5
You have fifteen minutes to complete this stage.

Stage 6 - Grandmaster Council Hall
This stage takes place in Grandmaster Council Hall (610030600)
A member of the party must speak with Battle Statue (9201115) to summon a handful of monsters (Master Guardian (9400594) and Crimson Guardian (9400582)). Once those have been killed, talking to the Battle Statue (9201115) again will summon the five bosses of Crimsonwood Keep (Margana (9400590), Red Nirg (9400591), Rellik (9400592), Hsalf (9400593), and Nagrom (???))). After defeating the four (or five) bosses, the party may proceed to the next stage through portal next00 via portal script glpqPortal6.
Nagrom must be introduced in later versions of MapleStory, not present in GMS v83.
You have one hour and thirty minutes to complete this stage.

Stage 7 - Grandmaster Secret Chamber
This stage takes place in Grandmaster Secret Chamber (610030700)
The party may proceed to the bonus stage through the portal next00 via portal script glpqPortal7.

Stage 8 - Bonus
This stage takes place in Crimsonwood Armory (610030800)
The party quest has cleared, party members have a limited time (1 minute) to collect drops from chests (reactors 6102002, 6102003, 6102004, 6102005). When the time is up, the party is warped to the exit. Party members may exit the bonus early via portal out00.
