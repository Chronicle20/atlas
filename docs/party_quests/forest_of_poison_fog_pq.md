Forest of Poison Fog Party Quest
Requires at least 4 players to register. Participants must be between level 45 and 55.
Recruitment is started by talking to Ellin (2133000) in Deep Fairy Forest (300030100).
Exit map - Outer Forest Exit (930000800) exists.
You have 30 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Added in GMS Version 72

Stage 1 - Mouth of the Forest
This stage takes place in Mouth of the Forest (930000100)
The party must defeat all of the Poisoned Lord Tree (9300172) in the map. The portal east00 will open and allow the party to proceed via portal script party6_stage

Stage 2 - Diluted Poison Collection
This stage takes place in Deteriorated Forest (930000200)
The party must kill Poisoned Stone Bug (9300173) in front of the green poison tree (reactor 3002000) to allow them to drop Diluted Poison (4001162). If killed anywhere else on the map, they drop Concentrated Poison (4001161), which cannot be picked up. Once four Diluted Poison (4001162) are collected, the thorn bushes (reactor 3009000) can be destroyed by dropping the items on them. The portal east00 will open and allow the party to proceed via portal script party6_stage

Stage 3 - Portal Maze
This stage takes place in Forest of Haze (930000300)
The party must navigate the series of portals to reach Ellin (2133001) at the bottom of the map. She will warp the entirety of the party to the next stage.

Stage 4 - Purification
This stage takes place in Poisoned Forest (930000400)
Each party member must speak to Ellin (2133001) to receive 10 Purification Marbles (2270004). The party members must weaken the Poison Flower (9300175) and use the Purification Marbles to capture the monsters to turn them into Monster Marble (4001169). Once 20 Monster Marbles (4001169) are captured, they must be given to the party leader, who will then speak to Ellin (2133001) to complete the stage. Ellin will warp the party to the next stage.

Stage 5 - Jump Quest
This stage takes place in Forest's Empty Lot (930000500)
The party members must navigate the jump quest to destroy boxes (reactors 3002001 and 3002002) to acquire a Purple Stone of Magic (4001163). Once acquired, the party member in possession of the Purple Stone of Magic (4001163) must speak to Sprite (2133004) to complete the stage. The Sprite will warp the party to the next stage.

Stage 6 - Boss Stage
This stage takes place in Forest of Poison (930000600)
The party leader must drop the Purple Stone of Magic (4001163) on the boss statue (reactor 3001000) to summon the Poison Golem (9300180). The boss has three phases, with phase 2 being Charged Poison Golem (9300181), and phase 3 being Super-Charged Poison Golem (9300182).
Defeating the boss will complete the stage. Party members may proceed to the next stage via portal out00 via portal script party6_out.

Stage 7 - Reward Stage
This stage takes place in Ellin's Forest (930000700)
Talking to Ellin (2133001) will award the player 52000 experience, and a Altaire Fragment (4001198) and teleport them to the exit.

This Party Quest had attempt / completion tracking which translated to a Rank. The rank is determined by your fastest clear time being below a threshold, a minimum number of attempts, and your success rate being above a certain percentage. For S rank, the fastest clear time must be below 15 minutes, you must have a minimum of 100 attempts, and your success rate must be above 80%. For A rank, the fastest clear time must be below 17 minutes, you must have a minimum of 50 attempts, and your success rate must be above 70%. For B rank, the fastest clear time must be below 20 minutes, you must have a minimum of 30 attempts, and your success rate must be above 50%. For C rank, the fastest clear time must be below 24 minutes, you must have a minimum of 10 attempts, and your success rate must be above 30%. For D rank, the fastest clear time must be below 30 minutes, you must have a minimum of 1 attempts, and your success rate must be above 20%. Otherwise, you will receive an F rank after a minimum of 1 attempt.
