Guild Party Quest / Sharenian Ruins Party Quest
Requires at least 6 players, to register. All members must be a part of the same guild. An individual must register for participation to challenge. A Master or Jr. Master of the guild can initiate registration for the guild. Once the guild master initiates registration, participants have 3 minutes to register.
Recruitment is started by talking to Shawn (9040002) in Excavation Site <Camp> (101030104).
You have 90 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Exit map - Returning Path (990001100) exists.
If the number of players is less than 6, the party quest will be failed.

Stage 1 - Protector Rock Collection
This stage takes place in Valley of the Guard (990000100) or Entrance to the Remains (990000200)
Party members must break purple crystals (reactors 9202000 or 9202009) to collect Protector Rock (1032033) earrings. These must be worn for the remainder of the party quest, else that member will die.

Stage 2 - Statue Sequence Memory Game
This stage takes place in Door to the Sharenian Castle (990000300)
Party members must observe a sequence, illuminated on the statues (reactors) in the map, and repeat it by attacking the statues. Replicating the sequence three times is required to complete this stage. If the party fails any of the three sequences, the combinations reset, and they must attempt to replicate the sequences again.
Completing the three sequences will trigger reactor (9208003) and permit the party to proceed through portal next00 via statuegate_open portal script. This script will warp the party to Road to the Fortress (990000301) to proceed to the next stage.

Stage 3 - Hall of the Knights
This stage takes place primarily in Hall of the Knight (990000400)
Party members must collect 6 Longinus Spear (4001025) via completing puzzles in the connecting rooms (Middle Path (990000401), Room of Glory (990000410), Room of Courage (990000420), Room of Faith (990000430), Room of Pledge (990000431), Room of Justice (990000440)). Dropping the Longinus Spears on the gate (reactor 9208004) will allow the party to proceed through portal next00 via speargate_open portal script.

Stage 4 - Fountain of Wiseman
This stage takes place primarily in Fountain of the Wiseman (990000500)
Party members must collect (ideally 4 of each) Scroll of Wisdom (4001028), Medal of Valor (4001027), Jr. Necki Drink (4001030), and the Spoiled Food (4001029). The Scroll of Wisdom and Medal of Valor are collected by killing the Black Knight (9300022) and Myst Knight (9300023) monsters in the Fountain of the Wiseman (990000500). The Spoiled Food is collected by interacting with reactors in Center Banquet (990000501). The Jr. Necki Drink is collected by interacting with reactors in Wine Cellar (990000502).

The items must be placed in front of the 4 statues to match the combination. Speaking with Guard Statue (9040006) will inform the party if the combination is correct. The combination resets on 7 failed attempts, spawns monsters, and randomizes the combination again. If the combination is correct, the reactor (9208013) is triggered, and the party can proceed through portal next00 via watergate_open portal script.

Stage 5 - Underground Waterway
This stage takes place primarily in Underground Waterway (990000600)
Party members must collect Sharen III's Pants (4001031), Sharen III's Shoes (4001032), Sharen III's Top (4001033), and Sharen III's Crown (4001034) and place them on his grave to clear this stage. These are collected through 4 maze portal, which navigate a party member to  Waterway Maze (990000610) and End of the Maze (990000611), Waterway Maze (990000620), Waterway Maze (990000630) and End of the Maze (990000631), or Waterway Maze (990000640) and End of the Maze (990000641).
All four pieces of equipment must be dropped on the ground in front of Sharen III's grave to clear this stage.

Stage 6 - King's Corridor
This stage takes place in King's Corridor (990000800)
A party member must sacrifice themselves (by dropping the Protector Rock earrings) in front of the door (reactor 9208009). The party may proceed through portal boss00 via portal script kinggate_open or boss01 via portal script kinggate2_open.

Stage 7 - Boss Stage
This stage takes place in Ergoth's Throne (990000900)
Break the crystal (reactor 9201002) on Ergoth's Throne to summon Ergoth (9300028). He will drop Mark of Evil (4001035). The party leader must talk to Tiger Statue (9040010) with the Mark of Evil to clear the stage.
On stage completion, the guild is rewarded guild points depending on the completion time. All registered members will be warped to the bonus stage Sharen III's Treasure Wearhouse (990001000).

Stage 8 - Bonus Stage
This stage place in Sharen III's Treasure Wearhouse (990001000)
The party quest has cleared, party members have a limited time (40 seconds) to collect drops from boxes (reactor 9202012).
