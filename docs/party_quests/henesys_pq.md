Moon Bunny's Rice Cake Party Quest
Requires at least 3 players, above level 10.
Recruitment is started by talking to Tory (1012112) in Henesys Park (100000200).
You have 10 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Exit map - Back to Town (910010300) exists.

Stage 1 - Primrose Seed Collection.
This stage takes place in Primrose Hill (910010000).
Party members collect all of the different Primrose Seeds (4001095, 4001096, 4001097, 4001098, 4001099, 4001100). Drop them on the correct platforms (reactors 9108000, 9108001, 9108002, 9108003, 9108004, 9108005), and the Moon Bunny spawns.

Stage 2 - Rice Cake Collection.
This stage takes place in Primrose Hill (910010000).
Protect the Moon Bunny by killing enemies. Keep the Moon Bunny alive until all 10 Moon Bunny's Rice Cakes (4001101) are created and given to Growlie (1012114).
Party members are awarded 1600 experience when the stage is completed, and then warped to the bonus stage Pig Town (910010200)

Stage 3 - Bonus
This stage takes place in Pig Town (910010200)
The party quest has cleared, party members have a limited time to hunt monsters in the map.
Party members may leave the map at any time through dialog with	Tommy (1012113), or are warped out automatically when the time limit is reached.
