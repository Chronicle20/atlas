Herb Town Party Quest
Requires at least 3 players, levels between 55 and 100.
Recruitment is started by talking to Guon (2094000) in Over the Pirate Ship (251010404).
Exit map - Pirate Ship On its Way Out (925100700) exists.

Stage 1 - On the Way to the Pirate Ship
This stage takes place in On the Way to the Pirate Ship (925100000).
Party members must kill all monsters in the map.
The stage is cleared by speaking with Guon (2094002).
The party may navigate to the next stage by entering portal next00 via portal script davy_next0.
The party has 6 minutes to complete the stage.

Stage 2 - Through the Head of the Ship!
This stage takes place in Through the Head of the Ship! (925100100).
Talk to Guon (2094002) to summon the first wave of monsters (Lord Pirate's Enraged Mr. Alli (9300114)) and collect 20 Mark of the Rookie Pirate (4001120). Talk to Guon (2094002) again to summon another wave of monsters (Lord Pirate's Enraged Kru (9300115)) and collect 20 Mark of the Rising Pirate (4001121). Talk to Guon (2094002) again to summon a final wave of monsters (Lord Pirate's Enraged Captain (9300116)) and collect 20 Mark of the Veteran Pirate (4001122). Talk to Guon (2094002) again a final time to advance to the next stage.
The party may navigate to the next stage by entering portal next00 via portal script davy_next1.
The party has 6 minutes to complete the stage.

Stage 3 - Through the Deck I
This stage takes place primarily in Through the Deck I (925100200)
Party members must kill all monsters in the map. 

This stage contains two optional missions:
If you kill all enemies in The Area of 100yrOld Bellflower I (925100201), speaking with Guon (2094002) will summon a treasure chest. If opened via a Lord Pirate's Key to Chest (4031437) it will elevate the Lord Pirate's boss fight difficulty (and rewards).

You also have 1 minute (upon entering the map) to kill all enemies in Lord Pirate's Servant I (925100202). When the timer runs out, the party will be teleported back to Through the Deck I (925100200).

The party may navigate to the next stage by entering portal next00 via portal script davy_next2.
The party has 6 minutes to complete the stage.

Stage 4 - Through the Deck II
This stage takes place primarily in Through the Deck II (925100300)
Party members must kill all monsters in the map. 


This stage contains two optional missions:
If you kill all enemies in The Area of 100yrOld Bellflower II (925100301), speaking with Guon (2094002) will summon a treasure chest. If opened via a Lord Pirate's Key to Chest (4031437) it will elevate the Lord Pirate's boss fight difficulty (and rewards).

You also have 1 minute (upon entering the map) to kill all enemies in Lord Pirate's Servant II (925100302). When the timer runs out, the party will be teleported back to Through the Deck II (925100300).

The party may navigate to the next stage by entering portal next00 via portal script davy_next3.
The party has 6 minutes to complete the stage.

Stage 5 - Eliminate Pirates!
This stage takes place in Eliminate Pirates! (925100400)
Party members must kill all monsters in the map, and hunt for 4 Old Metal Keys (4001117) to close the open doors (reactors 2519000, 2519001, 2519002, and 2519003).
Speaking with Guon (2094002) after unlocking the doors, the stage is cleared.
The party may navigate to the next stage by entering portal next00 via portal script davy_next4.
The party has 6 minutes to complete the stage.

Stage 6 - Boss Stage
This stage takes place in The Captain's Dignity (925100500)
Party members must kill Lord Pirate. Depending on if any optional quests (the two Bellflower map missions) were performed, you may fight Peeking Lord Pirate, Angry Lord Pirate, or Enraged Lord Pirate.
Speaking with Guon (2094002) after defeating Lord Pirate, the stage is cleared.
The party is awarded experience after the stage is cleared.
The party has 6 minutes to complete the stage.
