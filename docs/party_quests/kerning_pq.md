Kerning City Party Quest
Requires exactly 4 players, levels between 21 and 30.
Recruitment is started by talking to Lakelis (9020000) in Kerning City (103000000).
You have 30 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Exit map - 1st Accompaniment <Exit> (103000890) exists.

Stage 1 - Ligator Coupon Collection
This stage takes place in 1st Accompaniment <1st Stage> (103000800)
Party members converse with Cloto (9020001) to identify how many Coupons (4001007) they need to collect in exchange for a Pass (4001008). Amounts are assigned at random, and do not repeat. The amounts are 8, 10, 15, 20, 25, and 35.
To complete this stage, the party leader must provide 4 Passes (4001008) to Cloto (9020001).
Party members are awarded 100 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 2 - Vine Combination
This stage takes place in 1st Accompaniment <2nd Stage> (103000801)
Three party members hang on individual vines, if they match the randomized combination on communication with Cloto (9020001), the stage is cleared. The combination is 3 digits of a possible 4.
Party members are awarded 200 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 3 - Kitten Combination
This stage takes place in 1st Accompaniment <3rd Stage> (103000802)
Three party members stand on individual platforms, if they match the randomized combination on communication with Cloto (9020001), the stage is cleared. The combination is 3 digits of a possible 5.
Party members are awarded 400 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 4 - Barrel Combination
This stage takes place in 1st Accompaniment <4th Stage> (103000803)
Three party members stand on individual barrels, if they match the randomized combination on communication with Cloto (9020001), the stage is cleared. The combination is 3 digits of a possible 6.
Party members are awarded 800 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 5 - Boss Stage
This stage takes place in 1st Accompaniment <Last Stage> (103000804)
Party members kill Curse Eye (PC) (9300002), Jr. Necki (PC) (9300000), and King Slime (9300003) to collect 10 Passes (4001008). The stage is cleared by providing the 10 Passes (4001008) to Cloto (9020001).
Party members are awarded 1500 experience when the stage is cleared.
Party members are awarded a random weighted item (see below) and warped to the Bonus Stage when they speak with Cloto (9020001) after the stage is cleared.

Stage 6 - Bonus Stage
This stage takes place in 1st Accompaniment <Bonus> (103000805).
The party quest has cleared, but the party may be able to hunt on this special map until they leave, or the overall time runs out. Dialog with Nella (9020002) will allow the party member to leave early.
