Ludibrium Maze Party Quest
Requires at least 3 players, levels between 51 and 70.
Recruitment is started by talking to Rolly (9103001) in Ludibrium (220000000).
You have 15 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Exit map - Exit of the Maze (809050017) exists.

Stage 1 - The Maze
This stage takes place in a series of maps Ludibrium Maze 1-16 (809050000-809050015).
Party members traverse the maze map and collect Entrance Ticket to the Ludibrium Maze (4001106). The party quest is cleared by providing at least 30 tickets to Pietri (9103000) in Ludibrium Maze 16 (809050015).
The party is awarded experience based on the number of tickets collected.

Leaving the Exit Map after completing the party quest, will award the party member a random weighted item.
