Ludibrium Party Quest
Requires exactly 6 players, levels between 35 and 50.
Recruitment is started by talking to Red Sign (2040034) in Eos Tower 101st Floor (221024500).
You have 60 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Exit map - Abandoned Tower<End of Journey> (922010000) exists.

Stage 1 - Ratz Coupon Collection
This stage takes place in Abandoned Tower<Stage 1> (922010100).
Party members traverse up, and back down the tower collecting passes from Ratz from Another Dimension (9300005) and Black Ratz from Another Dimension (9300006).
The stage is cleared by the party leader speaking with Red Balloon (2040036) and providing 20 Pass of Dimension (4001022).
The party is awarded 2700 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 2 - Box Coupon Collection
This stage primarily takes place in Abandoned Tower<Stage 2> (922010200).
Party members collect Pass of Dimension (4001022) by breaking boxes (reactors 2200002 and 2200003). One or more unlucky party members may be sent to Tower's Trap (922010201).
The stage is cleared by the party leader speaking with Orange Balloon (2040037) and providing 15 Pass of Dimension (4001022).
The party is awarded 3600 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 3 - Bloctopus Coupon Collection
This stage takes place in Abandoned Tower<Stage 3> (922010300).
Party members traverse up, and back down the tower collecting Pass of Dimension (4001022) from Bloctopus.
The stage is cleared by the party leader speaking with Yellow Balloon (2040038) and providing 15 Pass of Dimension (4001022).
The party is awarded 3300 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 4 - Darkness Monster Coupon Collection
This stage primarily takes place in Abandoned Tower<Stage 4> (922010400).
Party members must collect Pass of Dimension (4001022) from	Shadow Eye from Another Dimension (9300008) located in several Darkness in the Tower (922010401, 922010402, 922010403, 922010404, 922010405) maps connected to this stage.
The stage is cleared by the party leader speaking with Lime Balloon (2040039) and providing 6 Pass of Dimension (4001022).
The party is awarded 3300 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 5 - Class Portal Coupon Collection
This stage primarily takes place in Abandoned Tower<Stage 5> (922010500).
Party members must collect Pass of Dimension (4001022) from boxes (reactors 2202003) located in several Tower's Maze (922010501, 922010502, 922010503, 922010504, 922010505, 922010506) maps connected to this stage. Two portals have specific class requirements to complete.
The stage is cleared by the party leader speaking with Green Balloon (2040040) and providing 24 Pass of Dimension (4001022).
The party is awarded 3770 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 6 - Warp Combination Puzzle
This stage takes place in Abandoned Tower<Stage 6> (922010600).
Party members must use warps to navigate a combination puzzle to reach the next portal. Combination 133221333123111

Stage 7 - Rombot Coupon Collection
This stage takes place in Abandoned Tower<Stage 7> (922010700).
A ranged party member must kill monsters (Ratz from Another Dimension (9300169), Black Ratz from Another Dimension (9300170), and Bloctopus from Another Dimension (9300171)) on top portion of map. This will spawn Rombad from Another Dimension (9300010) in the bottom portion which drop Pass of Dimension (4001022).
The stage is cleared by the party leader speaking with Sky-Blue Balloon (2040042) and providing 3 Pass of Dimension (4001022).
The party is awarded 4620 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 8 - Box Combination Puzzle
This stage takes place in Abandoned Tower<Stage 8> (922010800).
Five party members stand on individual boxes, if they match the randomized combination on communication with Blue Balloon (2040043), the stage is cleared. The combination is 5 digits, of a possible 9.
The party is awarded 5040 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 9 - Boss Stage
This stage takes place in A Crack on the Wall (922010900).
Party members must kill Black Ratz from Another Dimension (9300006) in order to trigger reactor (2201003) and spawn Alishar (9500330). Among his drops is a Key of Dimension (4001023) which the party leader must provide to Violet Balloon (2040044) to complete the stage.
The party is awarded 5950 experience when the stage is cleared.
After the stage is cleared, and after a short period (5 seconds) the party is warped to the bonus stage Abandoned Tower<Bonus> (922011000).

Stage 10 - Bonus Stage
This stage takes place in Abandoned Tower<Bonus> (922011000).
The party quest has cleared, party members have a limited time (1 minute) to collect drops from boxes (reactors 2202004). When the time is up, the party is warped to the exit. Party members may have dialog with Pink Balloon (2040045) to exit the bonus early.

Leaving the Exit Map after completing the party quest, will award the party member a random weighted item.
