Romeo & Juliet Party Quest
Exactly 4 players, level range 71 to 85
Recruitment is started by talking to Romeo (2112004) in Zenumist - Hidden Room (261000011) or Juliet (2112003) in Alcadno - Hidden Room (261000021)
The PQs are the same, but each have their own maps.
You have 45 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Exit maps (926100700 and 926110700) exist.

Stage 1 - Mysterious Lab (926100000 or 926110000)
Party members must interact with Investigation Result NPCs (2112007 or 2112013 depending on the map) which will randomly award 500 mesos, 500 experience, Romeo's Letter (4001130), and Juliet's Letter (4001131).
  If the party member finds "the switch" when interacting with the NPC. A message "{{characterName}} pressed the switch, and a special portal appeared." is written to the party in blue text, and the stage is cleared. A reactor portal (2618005) appears allowing members to proceed to the next stage.

Stage 2 - Dark Tunnel (926100001 or 926110001)
Party members must kill all monsters
  Once all monsters are killed, the stage is cleared, and the portal script (rnj1_out or jnr1_out) allows the party members to proceed.

Stage 3 - The Unpleasant Lab (926100100 or 926110100)
Party members must kill Homunculus (9300147) to collect Suspicious Liquid (4001132). With the goal of collecting the liquid to fill the three Beaker reactors (2618000). They will leak if not filled all at the same time. Filling all three reactors will clear the stage. A reactor appears (2618006) allowing the portal script (rnj2_out or jnr2_out) to proceed to the next map.

Stage 4 - Special Lab (926100200 or 926110200)
Party members may speak with Romeo (2112006) or Juliet (2112005), if they have Romeo's Letter (4001130), and Juliet's Letter (4001131) respectfully, this will trigger a strong boss in the boss stage.
Party members must kill Neo Huroid (9300148) to acquire 4 Card Key (4001133) items.
There are two additional rooms in this Stage. Dark Lab 1 (926100201 or 926110201) and Dark Lab 2 (926100202 or 926110202), that must be "opened" by the Card Key items, they close after 5 seconds. You must use a key to open the door from the outside to let another party member in / out. Portal scripts (rnj3_in0 and rnj3_in1 or jnr3_in0 and jnr3_in1) handle the "enter" interaction. (rnj_201_0 and rnj_202_0 or jnr_201_0 and jnr_202_0) handle the exit of the dark labs.
  A party member with max haste (thief) and teleport (magician) must collect Zenumist's Experiment Files (4001135) or Alcadno's Experiment Files (4001134) from box reactors (2612003 and 2518000).
  A party members HP is depleted by 100 every 3 seconds when in these special rooms.
  Talking with Romeo (2112006) or Juliet (2112005) with the files, will clear the stage, and allow the party to proceed via reactor (2618007) portal script (rnj3_out)

Stage 5 - Yulete's Office (926100203 or 926110203)
  A party member must talk to Yulete (but all party members must not be too close). She will summon a bunch of Reinforced Iron Mutae (9300252) and Reinforced Mithril Mutae (9300253) to advance to the next stage. This will clear the stage, and allow the party to proceed via reactor (2618005) portal script (jnr6_out)

Stage 6 - Party Member Warp Combination Puzzle (926100300 or 926110300)
  Each party member must enter their own room Unit 101 (926100301 or 926110301), Unit 102 (926100302 or 926110302), Unit 103 (926100303 or 926110303), or Unit 104 (926100304 or 926110304). These rooms have a series of 10 levels, of 4 platforms each. One platform at each level will advance the user to the next level, the other three will return them to the start. No two rows can have the same correct platform, between all rooms. A portal at the top of the room will take all party members to the next stage.

Stage 7 - Entrance to Center Lab (926100400 or 926110400) then Center Lab (926100401 or 926110401)
  This is the boss stage. Frankenroid (9300139) or Angry Frankenroid (9300140) will spawn based on if the party previously collected Romeo's Letter (4001130) or Juliet's Letter (4001131) and turned them in to Romeo (2112006) or Juliet (2112005) in Stage 4. The party must defeat the boss, while keeping a friendly NPC (Romeo (2112005) or Juliet (2112006)) alive. Defeating the boss, and keeping the friendly NPC alive will yield additional completion experience.
