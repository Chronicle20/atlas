Mal Volence Quest
Historically only available in GMS v66?
Requires 6 players, above level 10.
You have 20 minutes total to complete the Party Quest

Stage 1 - Letter Collection
Takes place in Initiation (674030000)
The party members must collect 15 letters by breaking rocks (reactor 6741016) around the map.
Providing	Charles (9220020) or Milla (9220019) (unclear which) will complete the stage, and enable warp to Entrance to MV's Lair (674030100).

Stage 2 - Entrance to MV's Lair
Talking to Lara (9220016), Charles (9220018), or	Milla (9220019) will enable entrance to The Lair of MV (674030200) via portal in00 portal script enter_td.

Stage 3 - Boss Stage
Takes place in The Lair of MV (674030200)
Break the rock (reactor 6741001) in the middle to summon MV.
Killing MV will complete the stage. Talking to Milla (9220019) will, if a player purchased a ticket from the Cash Shop, teleport them to the bonus stage on completion of this stage. Otherwise, they will be teleported to the exit.

Stage 3 - Bonus Stage
Takes place in Treasure Dungeon (674030300)
The party quest has cleared, party members have a limited time (10 minutes) to kill monsters, and collect drops from boxes (reactors 6742014).
Talking to Milla (9220019) will allow the player to leave the bonus stage early.
