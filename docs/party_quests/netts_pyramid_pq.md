Nett's Pyramid Party Quest
Requires at least 1 player, with a maximum of 4 players to register. Participants must be level 40 or above.
Recruitment is started by talking to Duarte (2103013) in Pyramid Dunes (926010000).
This party quest has different difficulty levels based on the participating players' levels. Easy (levels 40-60), Medium (levels 46-60), Hard (levels 51-60), Hell (level 61+)
Added in GMS Version 81

Stage 1 - Monster Killing 1
This stage takes place in Nett's Pyramid (926010100, 926011100, 926012100, 926013100) depending on the difficulty level.
This stage ends in 2 minutes. When the timer reaches 0, the party will be warped to the next stage.
If the ACT bar reaches 0, the stage will be failed.
Party members may speak to Duarte (2103013) to forfeit.

Stage 6 - Rewards
This stage takes place in Shades of the Pyramid (926020001)
Party members will be graded on the number of kills completed during the party quest.
Party members may speak to Duarte (2103013) to either enter the bonus stage, or exit. If the party member chooses to exit, they will receive Pharaoh Yeti's Sapphire (4001322), Pharaoh Yeti's Ruby (4001323), Pharaoh Yeti's Emerald (4001324), or Pharaoh Yeti's Topaz (4001325) depending on the difficulty level.

Stage 7 - Bonus
This stage takes place in Tomb of Pharaoh Yeti (926010010)
The party quest has cleared, party members have a limited time (1 minute) to collect Pharaoh's Treasure Chest (2022615 or 2022618) from Pharaoh Jr. Yeti (9700019 or 9700029). When the time is up, the party is warped to the exit. Party members may exit via portal out00. Easy mode, 30 Pharaoh Jr. Yeti (9700019) spawn. Medium mode, 40 Pharaoh Jr. Yeti (9700019) spawn. Hard mode, 50 Pharaoh Jr. Yeti (9700019) spawn. Hell mode, 50 Pharaoh Jr. Yeti (9700029) spawn.
