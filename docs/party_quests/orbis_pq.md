Orbis Party Quest
Requires exactly 6 players, levels between 51 and 70. 
If the party is composed of one of each job class, Bowman, Warrior, Magician, Thief, and Pirate. A random consumable buff effect is applied to the party. (2022090, 2022091, 2022092, 2022093).
Recruitment is started by talking to Wonky the Fairy (2013000) in The Unknown Tower (200080101).
You have 60 minutes total to complete the Party Quest
Exit map - Tower of Goddess <On the Way Out> (920011200) exists.

Stage 1 - Cloud Piece Collection
This stage takes place in Tower of Goddess <Entrance> (920010000).
Party members collect Cloud Pieces (4001063) dropped by clouds (reactors 2002001). Drop the Cloud Pieces on the pedestal in front of the glowing orb (reactors 2006000). Chamberlain Eak will appear, and you can proceed to the next stage through the portal to Tower of Goddess <Center Tower> (920010100).
The party is awarded 5000 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 2 - Box Coupon Collection
This stage takes place in Tower of Goddess <Lobby> (920010400).
Party members must complete the correct jump quest to collect the correct LP given the day of the week. Sunday - LP - Operatic Music (4001056), Monday - LP - Cute Music (4001057), Tuesday - LP - Scary Music (4001058), Wednesday - LP - Fun Music (4001059), Thursday - LP - Sad Music (4001060), Friday - LP - Cold Music (4001061), or Saturday - LP - Tight Music (4001062). Drop the correct record on the record player (reactor 2008006), and a box will spawn to collect the Statue of Goddess Piece.
The party is awarded 7500 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 3 - Sealed Room Combination Puzzle
This stage takes place in Tower of Goddess <Sealed Room> (920010500)
Party members (5) must occupy three platforms. If you do not get it, in seven attempts, you must leave and re-enter. A box will spawn at the top of the room to collect the Statue of Goddess Piece. The combination is 3 digits, but requires a sum total of the digits to equal 5.
The party may check with 	Chamberlain Eak (2013001) to verify the combination attempt. 
A box will spawn to collect the Statue of Goddess Piece.
The party is awarded 7500 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 4 - Cellion Puzzle
This stage takes place in Tower of Goddess <Storage> (920010300)
A Cellion in Tower of Goddess (9300040) will spawn on a platform. Once killed, it will spawn on another platform. On the 15th kill, it will drop a Statue of Goddess Piece.

Stage 5 - Small Piece Item Collection
This stage takes place primarily in Tower of Goddess <Lounge> (920010600)
Party members must enter (3) portals to navigate to Tower of Goddess <Room 301> (920010601), Tower of Goddess <Room 302> (920010602), or Tower of Goddess <Room 303> (920010603) and kill either Cellion in Tower of Goddess (9300041), Grupin in Tower of Goddess (9300042), or Lioner in Tower of Goddess (9300043) to collect 10 Small Piece (4001052) each. Another member (or two) should enter the bottom portal to enter Tower of Goddess <Room 304> (920010604) break boxes (reactors 2002002) to collect 10 Small Piece (4001052) each. All forty Small Pieces are collected and provided to Chamberlain Eak (2013001) will provide a Statue of Goddess Piece.
The party is awarded 7500 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 6 - Kill Forty Pixies
This stage takes place in Tower of Goddess <Walkway> (920010200)
Party members must kill forty pixies to retrieve forty Small Piece (4001051). All fourty Small Pieces (4001051) are collected and provided to Chamberlain Eak (2013001) will provide a Statue of Goddess Piece.
The party is awarded 7500 experience when the stage is cleared.

Stage 7 - Warp and Lever Combination Puzzle
This stage takes place in Tower of Goddess <On the Way Up> (920010700)
Party members must navigate the jump quest, use warps to navigate a combination puzzle, and complete a combination puzzle (triggering lever reactors 2008007 to match a 5 digit combination) to clear the stage. The party leader should speak to Chamberlain Eak (2013001) to verify the combination. A box will spawn to collect the Statue of Goddess Piece.

Stage 8 - Statue Repair
This stage primarily takes place in Tower of Goddess <Center Tower> (920010100)
Party members drop the statue pieces on the respective platforms to trigger the reactors (	2008000, 2008001, 2008002, 2008003, 2008004, 2008005). Talking to Chamberlain Eak (2013001) will complete the stage, and allow entrance to the next stage.

Stage 9 - Boss Stage
This stage takes place in Tower of Goddess <Garden> (920010800)
Party members must kill Nependeath in Tower of Goddess (9300048). They drop a Strange Seed (4001053) that must be put on one of the pots. The pot may trigger nothing, another normal Nependeath in Tower of Goddess (9300048) spawn, or a Royal Nependeath in Tower of Goddess (9300049). Killing the Royal Nependeath in Tower of Goddess (9300049) will trigger Papa Pixie (9500351) to spawn. Killing Papa Pixie (9500351) will cause a Even Stranger Seed (4001054) to drop. Place this on a pot, to trigger grass to grow, which drops the Grass of Life (4001055). Drop the Grass of Life on the statue to rescue Minerva. Minerva will thank the party for their help and warp the party to the next stage.

Stage 10 - Bonus Stage
This stage takes place in Tower of Goddess <Treasure Storage> (920011100)
The party quest has cleared, party members have a limited time (1 minute) to collect drops from boxes (reactors 2002014). When the time runs out, the party will be warped to the exit of the party quest.
